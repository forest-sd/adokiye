Behind the Scenes: Making of Glory ft. Sym19

The Inspiration Behind "Glory"

In the spirit of Christmas and New Year, all we can say is Glory, Glory, Glory to God Almighty for all His blessings! That's exactly how "Glory" was born — from a place of deep gratitude and celebration.

As the holiday season approached, I found myself reflecting on everything that had happened throughout the year. The highs, the lows, the moments of doubt, and the incredible breakthroughs. Through it all, one thing remained constant — God's faithfulness.

Collaborating with Sym19

When I thought about who should feature on this track, Sym19 immediately came to mind. His vocal style and spiritual energy were exactly what the song needed. When we got together in the studio, the chemistry was undeniable.

We spent hours working on the harmonies, making sure every note carried the weight of our gratitude. The recording session felt more like a worship session — there were moments when we just had to stop and give thanks right there in the booth.

The Recording Process

Creating "Glory" was a labor of love. We wanted the production to reflect the grandeur of what we were celebrating — God's glory. The beat needed to be uplifting, the melodies needed to soar, and the lyrics needed to touch hearts.

Our producer understood the vision perfectly. He layered rich African percussion with contemporary sounds, creating a track that feels both timeless and fresh. The result is a song that makes you want to dance and worship at the same time.

The Message

"Glory" is more than just a song — it's a declaration. It's a reminder that no matter what we face, there's always a reason to give thanks.

"Please subscribe to GLORY as your caller tune and watch how you'll move from GLORY to GLORY!"
