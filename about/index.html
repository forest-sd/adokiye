About Adokiye

Nigerian Afro-Fusion Artist • Singer • Songwriter • Philanthropist

The Artist

Born Adokiye Ngozi Kyrian, music breathes life into any troubled soul — but some people breathe life into music. Growing up in Nigeria in a Christian family, Adokiye's musical journey was deeply influenced by the powerful harmonies and uplifting messages of gospel music in Imo State and Rivers State.

Ever since her emergence, Singer Adokiye has been making the headlines of entertainment portals. With a unique blend of traditional African sounds and contemporary beats, she has carved out a distinctive space in the Afro-fusion genre.

The Music

Adokiye's discography spans gospel-infused celebrations to energetic dance tracks. Key releases include:

  • "Glory" ft. Sym19 — A Christmas celebration of faith and gratitude
  • "Back Up" — An energetic single with an official music video
  • "Love U Better" ft. Addiction — A soulful collaboration
  • "Pay As You Go" — A track with massive blog and radio play
  • "Body To Body" — Released under GHP Records (London-based label)
  • "Baby Baby" ft. Olamide — A crowd-moving collaboration

The Mission

At Adokiye, we believe in the power of creativity and innovation. Our commitment to excellence drives us to push boundaries and explore new possibilities — not just in music, but in community impact through the ADOCHANGE charity program.

Values

  • Innovation: Embracing change and seeking new ways to blend tradition with modern sounds
  • Collaboration: Working with artists, producers, and communities to create something greater
  • Integrity: Conducting everything with transparency and honesty
  • Excellence: Delivering high-quality music and community programs
